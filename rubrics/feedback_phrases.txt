FEEDBACK PHRASES FOR ASAP 2.0 ESSAYS (Score 1-6)
 
FOR SCORE 5-6 (STRONG MASTERY):
Your essay effectively develops a clear point of view with strong
critical thinking. Your use of evidence from the source text is
appropriate and well integrated. Your writing is well organised
with coherent progression of ideas. Vocabulary is accurate and
varied with effective sentence structure.
 
FOR SCORE 3-4 (DEVELOPING MASTERY):
Your essay develops a point of view but could use stronger evidence
from the source text to support your claims. Organisation is present
but transitions between ideas could be smoother. Consider using more
specific vocabulary and varying your sentence structure. Review your
grammar and mechanics before submitting.
 
FOR SCORE 1-2 (LIMITED MASTERY):
Your essay needs a clearer thesis — state your position directly in
the introduction. Use specific evidence from the source text to support
each claim. Work on organising your ideas: introduction makes a claim,
body supports it with source evidence, conclusion wraps up. Focus on
complete sentences and check for grammar errors that affect meaning.
 
COMMON WEAKNESSES TO ADDRESS:
- Thesis restates the prompt rather than arguing a position
- Claims made without evidence from the source text
- Evidence quoted but not explained or connected to the argument
- Poor organisation with no clear paragraph structure
- Grammar errors that seriously obscure meaning
- Very limited vocabulary or incorrect word choice
